# Areas

This folder contains **ongoing responsibilities** and areas of your freelance business that require sustained attention but don't have specific end dates.

## What Goes Here

- Ongoing responsibilities without completion dates
- Standards and processes you maintain
- Areas of interest you're developing
- Long-term commitments that need regular attention

## What Doesn't Go Here

- Projects with specific deadlines (those go in **Projects**)
- Reference materials (those go in **Resources**)
- Inactive areas (those go in **Archive**)

## Core Business Areas

### 1. Business Operations
Day-to-day business management, administrative tasks, and operational processes.

### 2. Client Relations
Building and maintaining client relationships, communication standards, and satisfaction tracking.

### 3. Finance
Financial management, budgeting, tax planning, and business financial health.

### 4. Professional Development
Skill building, learning, career growth, and staying current in your field.

## Organization

Each area should contain:
- Overview and purpose
- Standards and processes
- Regular review notes
- Related resources and links
- Action items and improvements

## Review Cadence

- **Weekly:** Active areas needing frequent attention
- **Monthly:** Most areas for health check
- **Quarterly:** Strategic review and adjustments
- **Annual:** Comprehensive area evaluation

## Areas vs. Projects

| Areas | Projects |
|-------|----------|
| Ongoing | Defined end date |
| Standards to maintain | Goals to achieve |
| No "done" state | Clear completion |
| Regular review | Progress tracking |

## Best Practices

- Review areas monthly for health
- Link projects to relevant areas
- Document standards and processes
- Track improvements over time
- Archive inactive areas
