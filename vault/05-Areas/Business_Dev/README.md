## Business Development Strategies

### Outbound
- **LinkedIn**: 10 targeted messages/week to ideal clients
- **Email**: Warm intros from existing clients
- **Content**: Technical blog posts, case studies
- **Events**: 1 conference/quarter, local meetups

### Inbound
- **Portfolio**: Keep `career_server` updated
- **Referrals**: Ask satisfied clients
- **Website**: Professional site with case studies
- **SEO**: Optimize for "freelance [your specialty]"

### Retention
- **Quarterly Check-ins**: All active clients
- **Value Add**: Share relevant articles, intros
- **Expand Services**: Identify new needs
- **Renewals**: Start conversation 60 days early

## Win/Loss Analysis
After every proposal, document:

**WON**: Tech Startup Inc. - $18K
- **Why**: Strong technical fit, competitive rate, quick response
- **Learnings**: Portfolio projects were key differentiator
- **Repeat**: Lead with portfolio in first call

**LOST**: Enterprise Corp - $50K
- **Why**: Went with agency, wanted on-site resource
- **Learnings**: Can't compete on enterprise scale
- **Adjust**: Focus on mid-size companies, remote-friendly
