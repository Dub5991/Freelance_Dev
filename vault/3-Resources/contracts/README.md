# Contracts & Legal Documents

This folder contains legal templates, agreements, and contract resources for your freelance business.

## Templates Available

### Service Agreements
- **Independent Contractor Agreement** - Standard service agreement
- **Master Service Agreement (MSA)** - Framework for ongoing work
- **Statement of Work (SOW)** - Project-specific terms

### Confidentiality
- **Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA)** - One-way and mutual versions
- **Confidentiality Clause** - For inclusion in other agreements

### Intellectual Property
- **IP Assignment Agreement** - Transfer of IP rights
- **IP License Agreement** - Licensing terms for work product

## Using These Templates

⚠️ **Important Legal Disclaimer**

These templates are provided for reference only. Always consult with a qualified attorney to:
- Review templates before use
- Customize for your specific situation
- Ensure compliance with local laws
- Protect your legal interests

## Customization Checklist

Before using any contract template:
- [ ] Replace all bracketed placeholders [Like This]
- [ ] Review payment terms
- [ ] Verify jurisdiction and governing law
- [ ] Customize scope and deliverables
- [ ] Add specific terms for your business
- [ ] Have attorney review if significant project
- [ ] Get both parties to sign

## Key Contract Elements

### Essential Clauses
1. **Parties** - Clear identification of both parties
2. **Scope of Work** - Detailed description of services
3. **Compensation** - Payment terms and schedule
4. **Timeline** - Start date, milestones, deadline
5. **IP Rights** - Ownership of deliverables
6. **Confidentiality** - Protection of sensitive information
7. **Termination** - How either party can end agreement
8. **Liability** - Limitation of liability clauses
9. **Dispute Resolution** - How conflicts are handled
10. **Signatures** - Binding agreement

### Payment Terms
- Payment schedule (upfront, milestones, completion)
- Payment method (wire, check, ACH, credit card)
- Late payment penalties
- Expenses and reimbursement
- Rate for additional work

### Scope Management
- Included deliverables
- Explicitly excluded items
- Change request process
- Additional work billing
- Approval process

## Contract Workflow

1. **Initial Discussion** - Understand client needs
2. **Proposal** - Send proposal with terms
3. **Negotiation** - Discuss and refine terms
4. **Contract Draft** - Prepare formal agreement
5. **Review Period** - Allow client to review
6. **Revisions** - Incorporate feedback
7. **Execution** - Both parties sign
8. **Storage** - Keep signed copy securely

## Storage & Organization

### Naming Convention
```
[Client-Name]_[Document-Type]_[Date]_[Status].pdf
Example: AcmeCorp_MSA_2024-01-15_Signed.pdf
```

### Status Labels
- Draft
- UnderReview
- Negotiation
- Signed
- Expired
- Terminated

## Renewal & Expiration

- Set calendar reminders 30 days before expiration
- Review terms for potential updates
- Reach out to client about renewal
- Update rates if appropriate

## Red Flags to Avoid

❌ **Never sign if:**
- Unlimited liability
- No payment terms
- Unclear scope
- IP ownership unclear
- No termination clause
- Unfavorable payment terms
- Requires working for free
