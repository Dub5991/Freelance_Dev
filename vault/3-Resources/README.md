# Resources

This folder contains **reference materials**, templates, and resources that support your projects and areas but aren't tied to any specific timeline.

## What Goes Here

- Reference materials you want to keep
- Templates and boilerplates
- Tools and guides
- Contracts and legal documents
- Research and learning materials
- Inspiration and examples

## What Doesn't Go Here

- Active project files (those go in **Projects**)
- Ongoing area materials (those go in **Areas**)
- Old/inactive materials (those go in **Archive**)

## Organization

Resources are organized by type and topic for easy retrieval:

### Contracts
Legal agreements, contract templates, and terms of service

### Templates
Reusable templates for common documents and workflows

### Tools
Tool documentation, tutorials, and configuration guides

## Best Practices

- **Name files descriptively** - "client-proposal-template-v2" not "template1"
- **Version templates** - Track changes and improvements
- **Add metadata** - Include date created, last updated, purpose
- **Regular cleanup** - Archive outdated resources annually
- **Cross-reference** - Link resources from relevant projects/areas
