# Finance

This area covers financial management, planning, and optimization for your freelance LLC.

## Purpose

Maintain financial health, optimize profitability, ensure tax compliance, and make informed financial decisions.

## Key Responsibilities

- **Revenue Management**
  - Invoice tracking
  - Payment collection
  - Revenue forecasting
  - Rate optimization

- **Expense Management**
  - Expense tracking and categorization
  - Cost optimization
  - Vendor management
  - Budget adherence

- **Tax Planning**
  - Quarterly estimated taxes
  - Deduction tracking
  - Tax document organization
  - CPA coordination

- **Financial Reporting**
  - Profit & loss statements
  - Cash flow analysis
  - Budget vs. actuals
  - Financial dashboards

## Monthly Financial Checklist

- [ ] Review all invoices (paid, outstanding, overdue)
- [ ] Categorize and record expenses
- [ ] Reconcile accounts
- [ ] Update budget tracking
- [ ] Review cash flow
- [ ] Check quarterly tax estimates

## Quarterly Tax Checklist

- [ ] Calculate quarterly revenue
- [ ] Calculate deductible expenses
- [ ] Compute estimated tax payment
- [ ] Make payment to IRS/state
- [ ] Document payment confirmation
- [ ] Update annual tax projection

## Annual Tax Checklist

- [ ] Gather all 1099s and income statements
- [ ] Compile expense documentation
- [ ] Organize receipts by category
- [ ] Calculate home office deduction
- [ ] Calculate vehicle expenses
- [ ] Gather retirement contributions
- [ ] Meet with CPA
- [ ] File taxes before deadline
