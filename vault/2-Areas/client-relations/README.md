# Client Relations

This area focuses on building and maintaining strong client relationships.

## Purpose

Develop and nurture client relationships that lead to repeat business, referrals, and long-term success.

## Key Responsibilities

- **Communication Management**
  - Response time standards
  - Update frequency
  - Communication channels
  - Status reporting

- **Relationship Building**
  - Regular check-ins
  - Value-added interactions
  - Personal touches
  - Long-term partnerships

- **Client Satisfaction**
  - Feedback collection
  - Issue resolution
  - Expectation management
  - Service improvements

## Communication Standards

### Response Times
- **Urgent:** Within 2 hours
- **Important:** Within 24 hours
- **Regular:** Within 48 hours

### Update Frequency
- **Active Projects:** Weekly status updates
- **Long-term Clients:** Monthly check-ins
- **Inactive Clients:** Quarterly touchpoint

### Channels
- **Email:** Primary business communication
- **Slack/Teams:** Active project collaboration
- **Phone/Video:** Weekly meetings and important discussions
- **In-Person:** Kickoffs and major milestones

## Client Health Monitoring

### Health Score Factors
- Recent communication frequency

- Project satisfaction

## Client Feedback Process

1. **During Project**
   - Weekly check-ins
   - Mid-project survey
   - Issue tracking

2. **Post-Project**
   - Completion survey
   - Testimonial request
   - Improvement discussion

3. **Quarterly**
   - Relationship health review
   - Future opportunities discussion

## Relationship Building Activities

- Monthly value-add emails (insights, articles, resources)
- Quarterly business check-ins
- Holiday greetings and acknowledgments
- Celebrate client successes
- Proactive problem-solving

## Red Flags to Watch

- [ ] Delayed responses
- [ ] Payment issues
- [ ] Scope creep without discussion
- [ ] Decreasing satisfaction scores
- [ ] Lack of future project pipeline

## Success Metrics

- Client retention rate
- Net Promoter Score (NPS)
- Referral rate
- Repeat business percentage
- Average client lifetime value

## Review Schedule

- **Weekly:** Review pending communications
- **Monthly:** Client health score updates
- **Quarterly:** Relationship reviews and outreach
- **Annually:** Client portfolio analysis
